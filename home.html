Welcome To National Puthoor

National Puthoor was chartered in 1974 with an aspirational young team who believed in Sports ,Arts Spirits and service to the community and. Since then Puthoor Area has done numerous Vollyball Tournaments apart from building a strong bond of friendship between them. Over the 48 years, the club has gone from strength to strength and had created innovative projects like Youth wing, Oruma koottayma, Volleball Tournament, Puthur Premeir Leagues and Run for life which are primarily fund raising projects to support the community service projects.
